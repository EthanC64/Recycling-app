Emmisions Tips

Helpful tips on emmisions and sustainable practices. (Click the blue titles for links on more info!)

Lowering Emmisions by Recyling

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Minimize waste and recycle materials.

Turn Off Lights

Energy and Water Conservation: Turn off lights, unplug devices, fix leaks, and use low-flow fixtures.

Bike or Walk for Short Trips

Try adding walking or biking into your everyday routine when heading to nearby places.

Use Renewable Energy

Renewable Energy: Consider solar panels and wind energy.

Unplug Devices When Your Not Using Them

Unplugging devices when not in use saves energy and money while reducing your environmental impact.

Keep Stuff Out Of the Landfill

Don't toss your stuff in the trash; instead, consider recycling, upcycling, or donating. It's a simple way to make a big difference for our environment.
